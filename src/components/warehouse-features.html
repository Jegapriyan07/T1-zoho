How Zoho Analytics delivers warehouse-native analytics

True warehouse-native analytics demands more than just a live connection. It requires the ability to query directly, blend intelligently, and perform efficiently at any scale, without compromise.

Direct Querying

Zoho Analytics connects live to your cloud warehouse and pushes query logic down for execution at the warehouse level. Supported across Snowflake, Databricks, Redshift, BigQuery, Azure Synapse, Athena, IBM DB2, Oracle Cloud, and more.

Cross Source Blending

Blend your live warehouse data with any other source, including business apps, databases, flat files, and feeds, within a single unified query model. This is made seamless through 500+ native connectors and a visual pipeline builder with 250+ built-in transforms.

Smart Caching

Zoho Analytics employs a hybrid result-set caching and query optimization layer that stores and serves reusable results transparently. The outcome is fewer redundant queries, lower compute consumption, and faster insight delivery.

Self-service BI and AI analytics, powered for your warehouse

Zoho Analytics delivers a full-stack BI and AI platform directly on top of your warehouse data, putting self-service analytics in the hands of every team.

Ask Zia

Ask Zia lets any user query live warehouse data in plain language and instantly receive answers as charts, dashboards, and narratives, in any language.

Automated Insights

Automatically generate actionable insights from your warehouse data without writing complex SQL queries.

Predictive Analytics

Forecast trends and anticipate future outcomes with built-in machine learning models applied directly to your warehouse.
